About

How I think, what I value, and the direction I’m building toward.

Who I Am

I am deeply interested in how decisions are made inside organizations — both in businesses and in public systems. What draws me in is not titles or roles, but leadership, structured decision-making, and how institutions evolve over time.

I see myself working at the intersection of the corporate and public worlds — solving business problems while remaining conscious of broader public outcomes and long-term impact.

How I Think

My approach to problem-solving is structured, fact-based, and exploratory. I rely on frameworks, data, and hypothesis-driven thinking, while accepting that no one can know everything at once.

I consciously study failures — why companies went wrong, where decisions failed, and what lessons can be learned. Understanding what not to do is, for me, as important as understanding best practices.

Why Consulting, Analytics, and Strategy

My interest in consulting and analytics grew from a deeper realization: I enjoy solving diverse problems in complex environments. Over time, I discovered that these fields offer continuous exposure to new industries, new challenges, and structured ways of thinking.

This naturally aligned with a growing desire to contribute beyond myself — to think about decisions not just in terms of outcomes, but in terms of responsibility and impact.

Learning and Sharing

Learning gives me deep personal satisfaction. I explore multiple domains, connect ideas across subjects, and constantly refine how I think. While this curiosity can sometimes feel overwhelming, it helps me develop perspective.

Writing blogs and creating content allow me to articulate my thinking, overcome hesitation, and share viewpoints openly. I strongly believe that knowledge without sharing is like a rock sitting idle for centuries — present, but unused.

Values That Guide Me

  • Sustainability: Decisions must consider long-term consequences.
  • Accountability: Ownership of outcomes, both positive and negative.
  • Trust: Meaningful work is built through strong teams.
  • Public Consideration: Every decision impacts people beyond numbers.

Long-Term Direction

In the long run, I aim to work closely with corporate organizations and eventually contribute to public institutions in India. My goal is to bring structured thinking, analytical clarity, and ethical consideration into spaces where decisions have lasting economic and social impact.

A Principle I Live By

I believe that we are not the same person we were even a second ago. No matter how the previous moment went, there is always an opportunity to rise, learn, and move forward.

Get in touch

I’m open to conversations around internships, early-career consulting roles, research projects, and content collaborations.

If you’re interested in discussing business strategy, analytics, retail operations, or public policy, feel free to reach out.
